Our Story

About Us – Chosen Care Collective

👩🏾

"Some caregivers treated her like family. Others treated her like a task. I never forgot the difference. That experience became my 'why.'"

— Founder & CEO, Chosen Care Collective

Chosen Care Collective was born from both experience and heart.

As a Black woman who has worked in healthcare and personally cared for my grandmother through years of complex medical and non-medical needs, I saw firsthand what true care should look like and how often the system fails our loved ones. My family depended on home care services to keep my grandmother safe, comfortable, and dignified. Some caregivers treated her like family. Others treated her like a task. I never forgot the difference.

That experience became my "why."

I saw the gaps in the industry:

  • Families overwhelmed and unsupported
  • Clients treated like numbers instead of humans
  • Caregivers undervalued and untrained
  • Agencies focused on profit instead of people
  • Cultural needs ignored
  • Quality and dignity inconsistent

I knew our community deserved better.

With my background in CNA-level care and professional experience in business, operations, and project management and alongside my husband, who also brings leadership and operational expertise we decided to build something different:

A care agency with both heart and excellence.

Chosen Care Collective is a Black woman-owned, family-led organization built on compassion, dignity, and high standards. We combine the personal touch of family care with the professionalism of clinical oversight and strong operational systems.

Our mission is deeper than providing services — we exist to restore dignity, support independence, and make quality care accessible to every community, not just those who can afford luxury prices.

We believe:

→

Care should feel personal.

→

Caregivers should be respected and supported.

→

Families should never feel alone in the process.

→

EVERY person deserves to be cared for and chosen.

This is just the starting point, from here we are building a full care collective. Our long-term vision is to build a full care collective that supports individuals and families in every stage of life providing whole-person care for the body, mind, and beyond.

Chosen Care Collective isn't just an agency. It's a movement to change the standard of care in our communities.

Your chosen care, our chosen family.

8 Reasons Families, Hospitals & Partners Choose Us

These are not marketing claims — they are the foundation we were built on.

1

Personal Experience + Professional Expertise

We understand care from EVERY angle — client, family, caregiver, and administrator. Our leadership brings both hands-on caregiving experience (CNA) and business/operations/project management expertise, allowing us to deliver care that is compassionate AND highly organized.

2

Black Woman-Owned & Family-Led

We are proud to be Black-owned, woman-owned, and family-operated. We represent the diverse communities we serve, build trust with families, and meet cultural needs that many agencies overlook. This also positions us for MBE, SWaM, and 8(a) certifications and government contracts.

3

Care That Feels Like Family

We don't just send caregivers — we build relationships. We believe in treating every client like chosen family with dignity, respect, and genuine love. This leads to better outcomes, higher satisfaction, and longer client retention.

Professional Standards & Clinical Oversight

While we start with non-medical care, our operations are built with RN guidance, strong policies, and high accountability. Our systems meet hospital, Medicaid, and VA-level standards from day one — unlike many small agencies that lack structure.

5

Commitment to Excellence & Integrity

We don't take shortcuts. We train, support, and supervise caregivers. We communicate with families. We follow through every time. We do what's right, even when no one is watching.

6

Culturally Competent & Inclusive Care

We respect cultural backgrounds, language preferences, spiritual beliefs, and family dynamics. Clients feel seen, understood, and valued — not treated like a number.

7

Strong Community Focus

We serve the communities we live in. We build partnerships with local hospitals, churches, community centers, and service organizations — creating a network of support.

8

Built to Grow & Expand

We are not just a home care agency — we are building a full care collective. Our long-term vision includes expanding into additional services to support the whole person: body, mind, and family.

Simple 3-Step Process

Care in 3 Easy Steps

1

Reach out to us by phone or through our contact form. Our team will answer your questions, explain our services, and schedule a free in-home or phone consultation — no pressure, no commitment.

Personalized Care Assessment

We conduct a thorough, compassionate assessment to understand your loved one's unique needs, preferences, daily routine, health history, and what matters most to your family. With RN guidance, we create a tailored care plan together.
